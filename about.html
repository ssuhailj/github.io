Welcome To SILVERLINE

Committed to Care

Silverline is a domestic pharmaceutical company that supplies wide range of capsules, tablets and liquids in India. It is based in Chandigarh and has wide operations in north India including Delhi, Punjab, Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, and Chandigarh.

We are committed to effective supply chain management, compliance to ethical code of pharma community, and high quality of our products. Silverline ensures compositional accuracy, optimum effectiveness and longer shelf life of its products. It is achieved by its partnerships with reputed manufacturing giants including MM Pharma, Aspen Lifesciences, Park Pharmaceuticals etc.

Our mission is to become the most valued Pharma partner to the World Pharma fraternity by continuously researching, developing and manufacturing a wide range of pharmaceutical products that comply with the highest regulatory standards. We have Quality, Ethics ands Commitment as cardinal principles of our edifice.
